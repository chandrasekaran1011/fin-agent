# Financial Domain Knowledge

## INR Currency Formatting

- Use Indian numbering system: lakhs (L) and crores (Cr)
- 1 Lakh = 1,00,000 (100K)
- 1 Crore = 1,00,00,000 (10M)
- Format: ₹1,23,45,678.00
- Abbreviations: ₹5.25 L, ₹12.30 Cr
- Always show 2 decimal places for financial amounts

## Indian Fiscal Year

- April 1 to March 31
- FY2025 = April 2024 - March 2025
- Q1: April-June, Q2: July-September, Q3: October-December, Q4: January-March
- Tax deadlines: advance tax quarterly, GST monthly

## Business Units

The company operates across 4 business units:
- **North**: Delhi NCR region operations
- **South**: Bangalore/Chennai/Hyderabad operations
- **West**: Mumbai/Pune operations
- **Corporate**: Head office, shared services

## Common Vendor Categories

- **IT**: Software licenses, cloud services, development
- **Logistics**: Shipping, warehousing, fleet
- **Manufacturing**: Raw materials, equipment, maintenance
- **Services**: Consulting, legal, audit, HR
- **Utilities**: Power, telecom, internet

## Key Financial Metrics

- **EBITDA**: Earnings Before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation, Amortization
- **DSO**: Days Sales Outstanding (average collection period)
- **DPO**: Days Payable Outstanding (average payment period)
- **Burn Rate**: Monthly cash consumption rate
- **Runway**: Months of cash remaining at current burn rate
- **Working Capital**: Current Assets - Current Liabilities
